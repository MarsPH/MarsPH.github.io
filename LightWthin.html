PROJECT OVERVIEW

Shadow Physics Innovation

3D world with 2D gameplay mechanics

3D/2D Hybrid System

At first glance, the game appears to be a typical 2D experience, but it actually takes place in a 3D world with constrained movement on the Z-X plane instead of the traditional X-Y plane. This unique approach creates fascinating spatial challenges and visual depth.

I developed a custom 2D physics system within the 3D environment where the shadows of 3D objects function as platforms for the player. This creates a unique gameplay mechanic where light and shadow become tangible elements of the world.

DEVELOPMENT STORY

48-Hour Innovation

From concept to shadow physics in two days

Hour 1-6

Concept & Research

Explored shadow-based gameplay mechanics and 3D/2D hybrid concepts. Researched shadow mapping techniques and Unity's rendering pipeline.

Hour 7-24

Shadow System Development

Implemented dynamic shadow mapping with raycasting and plane intersections. Created procedural mesh generation for shadow platforms with collision.

Hour 25-40

Gameplay Integration

Integrated shadow platforms into gameplay mechanics. Added light sources, player controls, and survival elements. Balanced difficulty and physics.

Hour 41-48

Polish & Optimization

Optimized shadow rendering performance, fine-tuned physics parameters, and added visual effects. Conducted extensive testing and bug fixes.
