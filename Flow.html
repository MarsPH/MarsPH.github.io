DEVELOPMENT STORY

48-Hour Challenge

From concept to completion in two days

Hour 1-4

Concept & Design

Brainstormed the bubble theme and physics mechanics. Decided on soft body physics as the core mechanic to differentiate from traditional rigid body games.

Hour 5-24

Physics Implementation

Developed the spring joint-based soft body system. Implemented vertex displacement calculations and stress monitoring. Created the procedural mesh system.

Hour 25-40

Game Systems

Built the complete game loop with obstacles, collectibles, and water flows. Implemented win/lose conditions, pausing, and menu systems.

Hour 41-48

Polish & Testing

Fine-tuned physics parameters, added visual effects, and conducted extensive testing. Optimized performance and fixed collision edge cases.
