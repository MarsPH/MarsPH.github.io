GAME OVERVIEW

High-Speed Interception

Precision flight and lock-on targeting

Core Gameplay

Pilot an interceptor through tight spaces while tracking and engaging targets. The prototype emphasizes readable motion, responsive acceleration, and snap-turn adjustments to maintain control at speed.

Systems explored include lock-on targeting, obstacle avoidance, and dynamic difficulty pacing. The aim is to create a satisfying loop with quick retries and high feedback density.

TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION

Systems & Mechanics

Unity-based gameplay systems

Lock-on Targeting

Target acquisition and retention with feedback cues for alignment and fire windows.

Obstacle Navigation

Tight-space navigation with acceleration and turn-rate tuning for precise control.

Difficulty Pacing

Scaling challenge via spawn timing, speed ramps, and reactive tuning.

Responsive Controls

Input smoothing and clamped banking for a snappy, readable feel.
