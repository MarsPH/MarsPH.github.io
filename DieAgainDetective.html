The Time Loop

Falkreath Village hides dark secrets. Every 50 years, children vanish, and a cycle of horror repeats itself. You're Detective John Doe, and you've stumbled upon an ancient evil—three occult families performing a ritual known as "The Chalk Circle."

They sacrifice three children to a nameless deity to preserve their power. You interrupt their ceremony, but it's too late—you're caught, and killed. But death isn't the end. You're trapped in a loop, waking again and again in your safehouse with only a chalkboard to record what you remember.
